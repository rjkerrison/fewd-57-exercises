The Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

My absolute favorite! These always made me feel incredibly happy when I'd go visit grandma. My sister and I loved helping her, but mostly, we just ate the cookie dough. :-p

As always with baking, try to be as precise as possible. Both with measuring, as well as the order in which you take the steps by.

Now get ready for some chocolately goodness.

Recipe by: My Grandma

Prep Time: 45 Min

Directions

First, gather all of your ingredients.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cup (3 sticks) softened butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 Tbl vanilla instant pudding powder
  • 2 Tbl milk
  • 2 Tbl vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 cups chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans(optional)

STEP BY STEP

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Beat butter and sugars together until light and fluffy.
  3. Stir in pudding mix milk and vanilla extract.
  4. Beat in eggs.
  5. Add dry ingredients and stir until combined.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts.
  7. Place 1 1/2 inch balls of dough 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet.
  8. Bake 8-10 minutes or until golden brown.
