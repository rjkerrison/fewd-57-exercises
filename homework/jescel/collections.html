CELARIS LONDON is a family-owned online store in England. Established in 2020 and is dedicated to produce the highest quality of handcrafted leather goods as possible. All of our goods are crafted by one craftsman and we use ethically sourced materials.

Please note that as leather is a natural material and each one of our products is completely handmade, colours and markings may vary from item to item. Each leather hide accepts dye differently so shade may vary slightly. The leather will transform with time, touch and sunlight to a rich dark tan.
